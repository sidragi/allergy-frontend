Pollen Allergy Prevention

Practical tips to reduce exposure and manage symptoms.

Indoor Tips

  • Keep windows closed during high pollen counts
  • Use HEPA air filters
  • Shower after being outdoors

Outdoor Tips

  • Wear sunglasses and hats
  • Avoid outdoor activities in early morning
  • Check pollen forecasts daily

Medical Tips

  • Start antihistamines before season begins
  • Consider allergy shots (immunotherapy)
  • Use saline nasal rinses
